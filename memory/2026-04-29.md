## What We'll Build
A comprehensive cost estimating system that calculates from components up (not sq ft averages), includes:
- Structure calculations by sq ft/story/foundation
- Material breakout by grade/finish level
- Gulf Coast specific factors (wind zones, flood, elevation)
- Labor category pricing
- Subcontractor flat rates
- Auto contingency buffer added
- Local supplier integration
