# Fiverr Seller Profile Strategy

## Target Customer Personas

### 1. The Busy Entrepreneur
- **Profile**: Startup founder, 25-40, bootstrapping
- **Pain Points**: Too many manual tasks, tech-averse
- **Budget**: $50-150 per automation
- **Needs**: Simple, reliable solutions

### 2. The Growing Business Owner  
- **Profile**: SMB owner, 35-55, established business
- **Pain Points**: Scaling challenges, disorganized workflows
- **Budget**: $100-500 per automation
- **Needs**: Robust, multi-step solutions

### 3. The Agency/Marketer
- **Profile**: Digital marketing professional, 25-45
- **Pain Points**: Client reporting, campaign tracking
- **Budget**: $75-300 per automation  
- **Needs**: Agency-specific tools
