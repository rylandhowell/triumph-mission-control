1. **AI Integration** - Few offer ChatGPT + Zapier combos
2. **Industry Specialization** - Most are generic
3. **Speed** - Quick turnaround times
4. **Education** - Teaching clients to maintain automations
5. **Maintenance Plans** - Monthly service subscriptions

### 5. Key Success Factorss
- 4.9+ star ratings
- 1,000+ completed orders
- Professional gig thumbnails
- Video demonstrations
- Fast response times (under 1 hour)
- Clear pricing tiers
- Strong portfolio examples
