## Brand Identity

### Mission Statement
"We help med spas and aesthetics practices recover 30%+ more revenue by automating missed calls, follow-ups, and appointment scheduling."

### Brand Positioning
**For:** Med spa owners struggling with missed revenue
**Who:** Lose money when calls go unanswered or leads go cold
**We:** Provide AI-powered automation that captures every opportunity
**Unlike:** Generic call centers or basic answering services
**We:** Specialize exclusively in aesthetics and understand the unique patient journey

### Brand Personality
- **Direct:** No fluff, straight to the point
- **Results-focused:** Everything measured in revenue recovered
- **Industry Expert:** Knows med spa operations inside and out
- **Local:** Understands Alabama market dynamics
- **Trustworthy:** Medical industry compliance aware

### Visual Identity Concepts
- **Colors:** Navy blue (trust), Teal (medical/aesthetics), White (clean)
- **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Logo concept:** Flowing lines representing patient flow + circuit patterns for automation

### Brand Voice
- Speak like a practice consultant, not a tech vendor
- Focus on revenue recovery, not features
- Use real numbers: "30% more appointments" not "better customer service"
- Alabama-friendly: Professional but approachable
