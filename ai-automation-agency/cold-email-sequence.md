# Cold Email Sequence - AestheticFlow AI

## Sequence Overview
5-email sequence over 14 days targeting med spa owners in Mobile and Baldwin County

### Goals:
- Start conversations with decision makers
- Book 15-minute demos
- Position as local expert in med spa automation
- Quantify the pain point before pitching solution

## Email Personalization Templates

### Replaceable Fields
- [First Name] = Owner/Manager first name
- [Spa Name] = Business name  
- [Local Competitor] = Similar med spa in area
- [Your Name] = Your name
- [City] = Mobile/Baldwin County specific

### Research Sources
Make each email feel personal:
- Check their website for services offered
- Google reviews for common complaints
- Facebook for recent posts about busy-ness/booking
- Instagram for appointment availability mentions
